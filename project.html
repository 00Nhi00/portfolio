My project for Thu Tuan Accessories Business

Background: Thu Tuan Car Accessories is a small retailer selling accessories for cars. They have been in business for 10 years but only sell at their physical store and do not have any online shop or do any marketing. Their customers are mainly loyal customers who have been buying from them for years.

Issues/Problem: It is getting harder for them to do their business when more and more competitors are selling and promoting their products online when they only have a physical store. They lost a large amount of income from the covid pandemic due to the government stay-at-home order.

Decisions: They decided to get a website to start selling online right away to be able to keep their business alive. They want their website to be simple but have pictures of the items they are sellings and their promotions

Work: The website was created with a homepage to tell the customer about their business, the shopping section that includes all their products and the promotion sections with all the sales items and discounts

Result: The business has increased their revenue by 30% since using the e-commerce website and been able to expand their product range and inventory to reach more customers.
